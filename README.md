# 🔒 The Linguistic Prison

**7 cells. 7 languages. 7 cognitive abilities you'll lose.**

An interactive dungeon escape where each cell is a real human language that removes a fundamental cognitive ability you take for granted. No numbers. No left or right. No past tense. Your brain literally can't function under their rules.

## The Cells

1. **Pirahã** — No numbers, no recursion, no fiction. 400 people who deconverted a missionary.
2. **Guugu Yimithirr** — No left/right. Only cardinal directions. Your spatial reasoning breaks.
3. **Hopi** — No past tense. Time doesn't flow — it accumulates.
4. **Kuuk Thaayorre** — Time flows east to west. Your timeline just rotated.
5. **Aymara** — The past is in front of you. The future is behind. Face what you can see.
6. **Russian Blues** — Two mandatory words for blue. You literally see colors differently.
7. **Tuyuca** — Every sentence requires you to cite your source. No evidence? Can't speak.
